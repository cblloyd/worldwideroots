Ras Kaya Paul

Founder/CEO

Ras Kaya Paul has over 20 years of experience in researching, breeding, cultivating, harvesting and distributing high-grade cannabis in Washington and throughout the world. As such, Kaya has developed a premier network within the industry to ensure information is shared and best practices are renewed. At his core, he is an educator. He is married with three young children and lives in WA.

Our Purpose

World Wide Roots is a cannabis seed company that aims to bring the highest quality genetics to the international seed marketplace. World Wide Roots begins all of its breeding programs with the benefits of the medical cannabis patient foremost in mind and designs a carefully curated experience for its customers. World Wide Roots represents more than a decade of collaborations through partnerships in the cannabis community and maintains a 50,000 seed inventory. Roots has worked with the leading advocates and geneticists across all aspects of the cannabis industry.
